First of all find a content before shooting a video. You need to clear what sort of video are you making. Gather necessary knowledge about the content that you are going to present.

It is most important thing that you need to be confident of what you are going to present with the audience. you need to be what you are going to present and how you are going to present with the audience.

After you completed the above task you can now upload your video. Later on you can monetize your channel to earn money from the youtube
